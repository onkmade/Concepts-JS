## How You Can Help

- Point out mistakes or bugs in the code.  
- Suggest better approaches or cleaner ways to solve problems.  
- Share simpler examples or explanations for beginners.  
- Add small, useful exercises or practice problems.  

## Guidelines

- Keep contributions simple and beginner-friendly.  
- Focus on **clarity** over complexity.  
- Use comments or examples to explain suggestions.  

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository.  
2. Make your changes or suggestions.  
3. Submit a pull request with a short description of what you improved or suggested.
